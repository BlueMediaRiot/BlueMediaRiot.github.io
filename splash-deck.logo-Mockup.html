SCENE / 01
CONCEPT 01

MAKE YOURSELF
AT HOME

Where design becomes personal.

A showroom is designed to help you imagine what's possible. But the best showrooms do more. They make you feel at home. In this campaign, we visualize that feeling literally.

We open tight on a moment that feels like home. A man in a bath. A woman with morning coffee. Then the camera slowly pulls back. And we realize, they're not home. They're in the busy Splash showroom.

WHY IT WORKS

The slow reveal does the selling without trying to. It turns the showroom into a place of ownership. Clean frames, long breaths, minimal language.

SCENE: "BATH TIME"

WE OPEN tight on a man sitting in a bathtub. Calm. Comfortable. Still. We hear faint showroom ambience. Footsteps.

The camera slowly pulls back. Revealing the truth. He is not in a home. He is sitting in a bathtub on display inside the SPLASH SHOWROOM.

CONCEPT 02

WE'VE SEEN
THIS BEFORE

Funny, human moments.

Walking into a kitchen and bath showroom can be intimidating. Suddenly, everyone becomes an expert. People pretend to know what they're doing.

We see couples confidently making questionable decisions. Then, calmly and without judgment, a Splash designer steps in. Not to sell. To help.

WHY IT WORKS

It taps into a truth most homeowners feel but rarely say: they're out of their depth. You don't need to have all the answers, because Splash does.

SCENE / 03
CONCEPT 03

DESIGNED WITH
CONFIDENCE

Design without the drama.

Designing a kitchen or bath can leave homeowners feeling... in the weeds. Too many options. We take familiar phrases like "in the weeds," and bring them to life visually.

We see a homeowner surrounded by actual weeds growing up through the floor as they stare at samples. Then Splash steps in. The weeds disappear. The vision clears.

WHY IT WORKS

The visual metaphor is instantly relatable. Elevated, not slapstick. It directly addresses a real fear: making an expensive mistake.

SCENE: "IN THE WEEDS"

WE OPEN on a woman standing in her kitchen. Samples cover the counters. She holds two nearly identical finishes, unsure.

VO
Designing your kitchen should be inspiring. But between the tile, the finishes... it's easy to get caught in the weeds.

Tall weeds now surround her. She pushes forward, parting the tall grass and emerges into the bright SPLASH SHOWROOM.
